Privacy & Safety

Built by a rider, for riders. No ads, no data-mining, and no corporate bloat. Just the tools you need to unlock your hardware.

01

Data Ownership: Local-First

Your training data belongs to you.

  • Core Metrics — Power, cadence, resistance, and heart rate are processed locally on your device to drive the HUD and Bluetooth broadcast.
  • No Hidden Tracking — We do not run background analytics, session recording, or sell your training history to third parties.
02

Game Leaderboards & Firebase

To provide a community experience in games like Solo Catamaran and Outrun, we use a secure Firebase backend.

  • What is Shared — Only your chosen nickname and game score are sent to the global leaderboard.
  • What is NOT Shared — Your weight, age, heart rate, or specific GPS/location data is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Opt-Out — If you prefer to ride in "Ghost Mode," you can choose not to submit your scores to the public board.
03

Device Safety & Permissions

VeloPlay and TribeView are built to respect your hardware.

  • Non-Invasive — Our apps run as a standard application layer. We do not "root" your tablet or modify the core Android system files.
  • Permissions — We only request access to Bluetooth (for sensor bridging) and Storage (for offline caching of lessons or map data).
04

Third-Party Integrations

When you use the Utility Browser to stream YouTube, Netflix, or News, you are interacting directly with those providers.

  • VeloPlay acts as a secure "window" for your content.
  • We do not intercept your login credentials or browsing history.
05

Updates & Security

To ensure your experience is always optimized for the latest v1/v2 hardware tweaks, the app may perform a lightweight version check upon launch via Firebase. This keeps your workflow running smoothly with the latest performance patches.
